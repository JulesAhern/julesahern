All About Jules Ahern

I am a first year student at the Cleveland institue of art and I am currently a painting major. I specialize in acrylic portraits and love to work with color and texture. I went to Victor Central Schools right outside Rochester New York and never saw myself going to art school until about Junior year. I thought that it could never be possible to have a job doing something you love so much.

I am in love with painting in everyway possible, but I have also done prints, drawings, sculptures, and digital peices. I am currently working on widening my horizons and trying new styles of art.
